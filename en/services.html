Services

House cleaning in Kunštát, Boskovice and the surrounding area

From regular household care to deep post-renovation cleaning — choose what you need, or get in touch and we'll put together a plan that fits.

Regular house cleaning

Weekly, bi-weekly or monthly cleaning for households that want order without the hassle. Includes:

  • living areas, kitchen and bathroom cleaning
  • vacuuming, floor washing, dusting
  • surface and appliance care
  • option to use your own products on request

Post-renovation & construction cleaning

After painting, flooring work or a full renovation, fine dust settles everywhere. I take care of:

  • removing construction dust from every surface
  • washing windows and frames after painting
  • deep floor cleaning
  • cleaning before move-in or property handover

One-off deep cleaning

Ideal before guests arrive, before holidays, or as a household "reset". We go through the spots that everyday cleaning usually skips.

Window washing & extras

Windows including frames and sills, oven and fridge cleaning, ironing on request — always available alongside your main cleaning.

FAQ

No, I normally bring my own equipment and products. If you prefer a specific brand or eco-friendly products, I'm happy to use those instead.

How far do you travel from Kunštát and Boskovice?

I work within roughly a 15 km radius of Kunštát and Boskovice. Not sure if your village is included? Send me the address and I'll check.

Can we arrange long-term cooperation?

Yes — that's what I focus on most. I'm happy to set up a regular schedule that fits your household.

Ideally right after construction work finishes, before dust settles into every corner. We'll agree on a date based on your renovation timeline.
